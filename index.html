Frequently Asked

Answers, before you ask.

Top three. Eight more on the FAQ page — or call dispatch and a human answers.

How far in advance should I book?

Anything the same day is possible with a 2-hour lead for sedans and SUVs, 4 hours for Sprinters. For weddings, corporate roadshows or holiday weekends we recommend 7+ days. Dispatch answers 24/7 — if we can do it, we'll tell you on the call.

What happens if my flight is delayed, early or diverted?

We track every flight in real time via FlightAware. If you're late, the meter doesn't start until you've actually landed — you're never charged for sitting in the air. If you're diverted to FLL or PBI, the chauffeur repositions automatically and we update you by text.

How is tipping handled?

A 20% gratuity is included in every quote — no awkward curbside math, no tip jar, no pressure. If the service exceeded expectations and you'd like to add more, the option exists on the emailed receipt.

What is your cancellation policy?

Sedans and SUVs: free cancellation up to 3 hours before pickup. Sprinters, weddings and hourly-as-directed: 24 hours. Flight cancellations and weather diversions are handled case-by-case — always in your favor.

Before you continue — about the parking pass

For stadium events, arena concerts, festivals and large venues, a venue-issued parking pass makes a real difference to your arrival and departure experience:

  • With a pass Your chauffeur waits at the dedicated vehicle pickup zone inside the perimeter. You walk out of the gate straight into the car. Drive away within minutes.
  • Without a pass Drop-off happens at the venue perimeter — often a 5–10 minute walk from the entrance. The chauffeur must leave to wait at an offsite lot. After the event, expect 20–45 minutes coordinating pickup and navigating post-event traffic.

Forward the pass to dispatch in advance, or tell us below and we'll coordinate venue parking on your behalf (additional fee may apply).
