What You Get

An account, not an app.

Everything an executive assistant needs to move principals with zero friction — built for people who book for other people.

◈

Dedicated account manager

One person who knows your principals, their preferences, the addresses, the difficult flights. Named cell, direct line, out-of-hours backup.

◷

Priority 24/7 dispatch

Account holders route to a separate desk. Last-minute changes, same-day stacking, redirects mid-flight — handled in minutes, not hours.

▦

Consolidated monthly invoice

One clean PDF at month-end with cost center, traveler name, route and date. Matrix reports on request. No curbside card-running, no dozens of Venmo threads.

◼

Multi-rider scheduling

Simultaneous pickups for offsites and board days. Multiple vehicles, same chauffeur assignment each trip where possible.

✈

Roadshow coordination

Multi-city, multi-day, one point of contact. We plan the ground side while your team runs the meetings — hotel to venue to FBO, back to back.

◉

Global affiliate network

Same standard outside South Florida. Vetted affiliates in every major US and international market, single point of billing, one point of accountability.

❖

Preferred corporate rates

Negotiated flat rates by route and vehicle. Volume tiers at 20, 50 and 100+ rides per month. Locked for the account term, reviewed annually.

⬩

NDA & confidentiality standard

Chauffeurs are trained in discretion by default — no names repeated, no routes discussed, no social media. Mutual NDA available on request.

◆

Custom billing & reporting

Cost codes, matter numbers, traveler tags, per-department summaries. Exports in CSV or directly into your T&E system.
