About Luna Executive Chauffeurs — Private Chauffeur Service in South Florida

SOUTH FLORIDA BORN  ·  EVERYWHERE YOU GO

The Standard.

Luna is a chauffeur service built in Miami and designed for the way serious travelers move. Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach — three airports, six counties, one dispatch running around the clock. When the trip takes you beyond South Florida, our affiliate network covers New York, Los Angeles, London, Paris, Rome, Milan, Dubai, Tokyo, São Paulo, and counting. Same booking flow. Same expectation. The car is different. The standard is not.

Every chauffeur is livery-plated, background-checked, uniformed, and trained in discretion — not as a checkbox, but as the floor. Clients who ride regularly get the same chauffeur when schedules allow. Pricing is fixed before you confirm: no surge, no route inflation, no fine print. Flight delayed? Your car waits. The app tracks your flight from takeoff. You do nothing. That is not a feature. That is the protocol.

The Difference

Three ways we’re different.

These aren’t marketing points. They’re the operating principles every reservation is built on.

01

Discretion is the default

We don’t ask you why you’re traveling. We don’t disclose your itinerary. Silence in the cabin is standard, not a request. Our chauffeurs are trained to read the room — a conversation if you want one, quiet if you don’t.

02

Coverage that follows you

South Florida is home, but we coordinate nationally and internationally through a vetted affiliate network. One call to Luna, same standard anywhere. Corporate accounts travel coast to coast without switching providers.

03

Technology in the background

Real-time flight tracking, live dispatch confirmation, digital receipts — all running quietly behind the scenes. You’ll never explain your delay or chase a car. The technology exists so you don’t have to notice it.
