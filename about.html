Operations

How Luna Operates.

Five steps, one protocol. Every ride moves through them.

  1. 01

    Request

    Client submits the ride or calls dispatch.

  2. 02

    Confirm

    Luna verifies timing, vehicle, chauffeur and rate.

  3. 03

    Monitor

    Flights, schedules and ride status are monitored when relevant.

  4. 04

    Chauffeur

    The assigned chauffeur arrives prepared, uniformed and ready.

  5. 05

    Complete

    The ride is handled with discretion, communication and consistency.
