Fleet FAQ

Quick Answers Before You Book.

Which vehicle should I book for an airport pickup?

For 1–3 passengers, choose an executive sedan (S-Class or Maybach). For luggage-heavy arrivals or families, choose the Escalade. For groups, choose a Sprinter, bus or coach. Luna Dispatch can confirm the best fit before pickup.

Can Luna recommend a vehicle for me?

Yes. Send passenger count, luggage count and occasion. Dispatch will recommend the right class before confirming the ride and the rate.

Are vehicles confirmed before pickup?

Yes. Luna confirms the vehicle, the chauffeur and the rate before the ride begins. No improvisation at the curb.

Do you offer group transportation?

Yes. Sprinters (7, 11 or 14 seats), a 30-seat luxury bus and a 50-seat charter coach are available for groups, events, weddings and corporate movement.

Are vehicles available for airport, corporate and event service?

Yes. Every vehicle in the fleet supports airport transfers, private aviation, corporate travel, weddings, events and point-to-point rides.
